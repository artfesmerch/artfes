How we started

Originally a passion project for NSTP, Art FES has since evolved to a collection of independent artists teaming together to form a collaborative shop.

Our artists

cosmic

Cosmic draws fan art for multiple fandoms. Her fandoms range from mainstream to niche. She has a trademark art style that consists of sharp line art and bright colors.

CrePe

CrePe or crenin but most popularly known as Cpn is an artist who started his art journey amidst the pandemic. He strives to give vibrancy in his brushstrokes in every illustration he does.

Frannyy

Frannyy loves to create pieces that contain pastel colors, hearts, and stars. She mostly makes fanarts from series that she likes, and seldom makes original art. She makes use of different patterns such as half tones, grids, and gradients as a way to make her art pop out more.

Kasu

With digital, straightforward illustrations, Kasu creates both original and fandom-inspired art. Her works are a mix of both simple yet complex elements, coloring and lining techniques are mostly influenced from how Japanese anime are drawn.

starrii

Starrii makes both original and fandom art. Her works feature bright colors that incorporate a touch of neons. Starrii has varying art styles that fit any mood or theme depending on the piece and loves to experiment new art techniques!

Our Identity

ArtFes is a group of 5 students formed in 2023. Their main goal is to create and sell their art through merchandise such as art prints, pins, stickers, and etc. This is a brand that offers different art styles that suits buyers who are looking for variety. Therefore, the triangles in the logo each represent the individual artists who have come together to form this creative circle that unites for the same goals and objectives in mind.
